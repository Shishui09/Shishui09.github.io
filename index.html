Grid Omega is an offensive hissatsu tactics that appeared in Inazuma Eleven Ares No Tenbin, based on a red giant hurricane who's gonna hurt everyone in the field. All the opposing team won't be able to play the rest of the match. This is a horrific and forbidden hissatsu. This tactics has been used by Outei Tsukinomiya to defeat Seishou Gakuen.
In the second season, Orion no Kokuin, Grid Omega as also been used by Guardians of Queen, the representative team of Italy. They tried to end the match quickly but failed.
Grid Omega have 3 phases :
In the phase one, The leader of the tactic show to everyone that he wants to use Grid Omega. In the phase two, ared wind appears on the field and spreads around the opposing players. In the phase three, a giant red hurricane appears and propels all the opponent in the air. At the end of the tactic, everyone falls to the ground and gets hurt, being unable to play the rest of the match.
